The Best Books/Book Series I've Read

My Top 3 Books/Books Series

Percy Jackson Series by Rick Riordan

This is a series based on Greek Mythology. Percy Jackson finds out that he is the son of Poseidon the God of the Seas. He then with his friends go on many quests to stop Kronos the Titan Lord from rising. I love this Series because I find Greek Mythology very fascinating and this book is a really good read because it is good to read about the different adventures the heroes go on.

Harry Potter Series By J.K Rowling

This series is about a boy whos parents were murdered. He goes on to find out that he is a Wizard and when he goes to Hogwarts (Magic School) he learns all types of Magic and finds out about his parents. This Series is very action-packed and it is one of the best books out there

Liars By Jack Heath

This book is about a boy who creates a lie detector but many people believe that the Lie Detector is rigged and they turn on him. If you like a good mystery book then this is for you.
